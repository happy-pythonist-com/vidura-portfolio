Why Vidura AI?

Vidura AI is a prompt manager designed for managing all your prompts targeting various AI generation systems, such as text-to-text, text-to-image, text-to-speech, and text-to-music platforms. With a centralized location for your prompts, it becomes easy to find, label, and copy them. Vidura AI offers a cloud-based platform where users can create an account within seconds and start working with prompts. Prompts serve as the source code for AI generation, making it essential to save, version control, and integrate them with different systems. One can think Vidura as a Content Management System(CMS) for prompts, but with super powers.
